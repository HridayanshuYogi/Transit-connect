# Transit-connect 🚍
⏰ Lack of Real-Time Information :- Most bus schedules are available only in printed form or static timetables. These schedules do not update according to real-time conditions such as traffic, accidents, or weather changes.

❓ Uncertainty in Bus Arrival Time :- Passengers often do not know the exact arrival time of buses. This creates confusion and frustration, especially during peak hours.

⌛ Time Wastage for Passengers :- Without accurate arrival information, passengers may reach the bus stop too early or miss the bus, leading to unnecessary waiting and loss of valuable time.

🚧 No Information About Delays :- If a bus is delayed due to traffic congestion, road construction, or technical issues, passengers usually receive no prior information.

🗺️ Difficulty in Route Planning :- New passengers or tourists may find it difficult to understand bus routes and decide which bus they should take.

📢 Poor Communication System :- There is no efficient communication system between transport authorities and passengers to provide updates about route changes or delays.

🚗 Preference for Private Vehicles :- Due to unreliable bus schedules and lack of information, many people prefer using private vehicles, which increases traffic congestion and pollution.

⭐ Conclusion : 
The above challenges show the need for a smart bus tracking and scheduling system (Transit-connect 🚍) that can provide real-time bus location, accurate arrival times, and instant updates to passengers, improving the overall public transportation experience.
